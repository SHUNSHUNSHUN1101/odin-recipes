Ingredients

    DOUGH:

  • 2 cups cashews
  • 1 cup old-fashioned oats (gluten-free if necessary)
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut, unsweetened
  • 1/2 cup dates, pitted
  • 2 bananas
  • 1 cup freeze-dried strawberries
  • 1 tablespoon hone

    • FILLING:

  • 1 cup vanilla yogurt (nondairy if necessary)
  • 1 teaspoon wheatgrass powder

Directions

Special equipment: a large straw or pastry tip

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Working on a well-floured surface, make a 1 1/2-inch ball of pizza dough, then press flat and spread into a circle. Place 1 teaspoon of the pizza sauce into the middle of the dough, then spread evenly over surface. Sprinkle 1/2 tablespoon of the mozzarella over on top.
  3. Using a large straw or pastry tip, cut out about 7 mini circles of pepperoni from 1 regular slice, then arrange the mini pepperonis on the pizza. Next transfer the completed pizza to a parchment-lined cookie sheet and set aside. Repeat with the remaining dough, pizza sauce, mozzarella and pepperoni.
  4. Once the pizzas are assembled, bake in the oven for 5 to 10 minutes. Be sure to keep an eye on these, as they can burn quickly. Serve and enjoy.
