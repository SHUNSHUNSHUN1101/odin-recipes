Ingredients

    DOUGH:

  • 2 cups cashews
  • 1 cup old-fashioned oats (gluten-free if necessary)
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut, unsweetened
  • 1/2 cup dates, pitted
  • 2 bananas
  • 1 cup freeze-dried strawberries
  • 1 tablespoon hone

    • FILLING:

  • 1 cup vanilla yogurt (nondairy if necessary)
  • 1 teaspoon wheatgrass powder

Directions

Special equipment: a small squeeze bottle or pastry bag fitted with a small tip

  1. For the dough: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a food processor, pulse together the cashews, oats, coconut and dates until well blended, 1 to 2 minutes. Transfer mixture into a bowl and set aside.
  3. Next, blend the bananas, freeze-dried strawberries and honey in the food processor until a paste forms, about 30 seconds. Once blended, add the date mixture and pulse together for 1 minute.
  4. Scoop out 2 tablespoons of the dough and form into an oblong ball with a divot in the center. Pinch the edges together slightly to “close the eye” but make sure not to seal them shut. Repeat with the remaining dough. Bake on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or a silicone mat, 15 to 20 minutes. Meanwhile, make the filling
  5. For the filling: Whisk together the yogurt and wheatgrass powder in a small bowl. Transfer to a small squeeze bottle or pastry bag fitted with a small tip. After the Eye Holes are removed from the oven and cooled slightly, fill the divot in the center with the yogurt filling.
